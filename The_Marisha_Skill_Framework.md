# The Marisha Skill
## A Growth Hacking Framework for High-Converting Funnels & Compliant Checkout Experiences

---

## What Is The Marisha Skill?

The Marisha Skill is a systematic methodology for transforming underperforming funnels into high-converting revenue machines. It covers landing pages, webinar funnels, email sequences, checkout pages, and post-event follow-up ‚Äî combining conversion psychology, premium UX, compliance requirements, and strategic copywriting into a repeatable framework.

**Core Philosophy:** Most funnels fail because they talk AT visitors instead of taking them on an emotional journey toward transformation. And most funnel operators think the conversation ends too early. On checkout pages, most fail because they try to be clever instead of clear.

**The Three Pillars:**
1. **Conversion** - Psychology + Copy + Structure that moves visitors to action
2. **Compliance** - FTC, accessibility, and billing disclosure that protects the business
3. **Consistency** - Every number, promise, and element must align across the funnel

---

## The Two-Crisis Framework

**Before diving into tactics, diagnose the real problem.**

Most funnels don't have 10 problems. They have 1-2 CRITICAL bottlenecks holding everything back. Everything else is "nice to have."

**How to identify your crises:**

| Metric | Crisis Level | Good | Excellent |
|--------|-------------|------|-----------|
| CPL (free webinar) | >$40 | $15-25 | <$15 |
| CPL (paid event) | >$100 | $50-80 | <$50 |
| Show-up rate | <20% | 25-35% | >40% |
| Landing page conversion | <20% | 30-40% | >50% |
| Revenue per attendee | <$100 | $150-300 | >$375 |

**Rule:** Find the 1-2 metrics at CRISIS level. Fix those first. Everything else unlocks after.

## The 5-Phase Framework

### Phase 1: AUDIT
*Score the funnel before you touch it*

**The 5-Dimension Scoring System (1-10 each):**

| Dimension | What You're Measuring |
|-----------|----------------------|
| **Structure** | Page length, information hierarchy, section flow |
| **Copy** | Headlines, CTAs, value propositions, testimonials |
| **Conversion** | Urgency, scarcity, social proof, friction points |
| **Psychology** | Emotional arc, aspiration vs fear, identity transformation |
| **Infrastructure** | Deliverability, follow-up sequences, backup channels |

**Landing Page Audit Questions:**
- [ ] How long is the page? (Ideal: 3,000-6,000px)
- [ ] Is there video content?
- [ ] Does the CTA contrast with the page?
- [ ] Is there urgency/scarcity?
- [ ] Do visitors know "who's in the room"?
- [ ] Are testimonials scannable (headlines, not just quotes)?
- [ ] Is the price anchored or hidden?

**Webinar Funnel Audit Questions:**
- [ ] Is there a video explainer on registration page?
- [ ] Does the thank you page auto-add to calendar?
- [ ] Is there a free bonus delivered immediately?
- [ ] Are there qualification questions to identify VIPs?
- [ ] Is there a WhatsApp/SMS backup channel?
- [ ] Are emails hitting inbox or spam? (Check SPF, DKIM, DMARC)
- [ ] Is there a post-webinar follow-up sequence?

### Phase 2: PSYCHOLOGY
*Understand what actually drives action*

**The Emotional Arc:**
```
TENSION ‚Üí PAIN ‚Üí HOPE ‚Üí BELIEF ‚Üí DESIRE ‚Üí FOMO ‚Üí ACTION
```

Most pages are flat. Great pages build to a crescendo.

**The 4 Psychological Triggers:**

1. **Aspiration Over Fear**
   - ‚ùå "95% of AI pilots fail" (assumes they identify with failure)
   - ‚úÖ "Join the top 5% capturing millions in AI value"

2. **Identity Transformation**
   - ‚ùå "Learn about AI" (information)
   - ‚úÖ "Become the AI-empowered executive" (transformation)

3. **Status & Belonging**
   - "Who's in the room?" matters more than "What's in the course?"
   - Show companies represented, industries, role breakdowns

4. **Loss Aversion > Gain Seeking**
   - ‚ùå "Attend and learn X" (gain-seeking)
   - ‚úÖ "Don't attend and LOSE access to $97 handbook" (loss aversion)
   - Loss aversion is 2X more powerful than gain seeking

**The Enemy Principle:**
Every great sales page needs a villain. Define what you're fighting against:
- The old way of doing things
- Competitors who are moving faster
- Consultants who overcomplicate
- Technology that doesn't deliver

**The 78% Rule (Hormozi):**

Pre-webinar content consumption is the #1 predictor of conversion. When registrants consume content BEFORE the webinar, their show-up and conversion rates skyrocket.

| Pre-Event Engagement | Expected Conversion |
|---------------------|---------------------|
| No pre-consumption | 2-5% |
| Partial consumption | 8-12% |
| 78%+ consumption | 15-25%+ |

**How to apply:**
- Deliver valuable content immediately on thank you page
- Send "homework" before the webinar
- Create a pre-webinar video series
- Track engagement and segment VIPs for personal follow-up

**Rule:** The sale happens BEFORE the webinar. Your job during the webinar is just to close what's already primed.

### Phase 6: COMPLIANCE
*The protection that keeps you in business*

**The FTC Negative Option Rule (effective May 2025):**

If you sell subscriptions, memberships, or any recurring billing, you MUST comply with the FTC Negative Option Rule. Non-compliance = fines, lawsuits, and refund liability.

**Mandatory Requirements:**

| Requirement | What It Means | How to Implement |
|-------------|---------------|------------------|
| **Clear disclosure** | Billing terms must be obvious before purchase | Show "$X/month, billed monthly" prominently |
| **Informed consent** | Customer must affirmatively agree | Checkbox: "I understand I will be billed $X/month" |
| **Easy cancellation** | Cancel must be as easy as signup | One-click cancel, no hoops, no dark patterns |
| **Confirmation** | Written confirmation of terms | Email receipt with billing terms |

**The Billing Disclosure Principle:**

> "If a customer can miss the billing terms, you've already failed compliance."

```
‚ùå WRONG:
[Buy Now $47]
*terms apply, see below for details

‚úÖ RIGHT:
[Subscribe - $47/month]
‚òëÔ∏è I understand I will be charged $47 monthly until I cancel
Cancel anytime with one click
```

**Checkout Page Compliance Checklist:**
- [ ] Billing frequency visible at point of purchase (not buried)
- [ ] Total price including any trials clearly stated
- [ ] Cancellation method explained before purchase
- [ ] Consent checkbox for recurring billing
- [ ] Order confirmation email with billing terms

**WCAG 2.1 AA Accessibility Standards:**

Accessibility isn't optional ‚Äî it's both legal compliance (ADA) and smart UX (10-15% of users have some form of disability).

**The 4 Principles (POUR):**

| Principle | Meaning | Implementation |
|-----------|---------|----------------|
| **Perceivable** | Users can perceive content | Alt text, captions, sufficient contrast |
| **Operable** | Users can navigate | Keyboard navigation, no time limits |
| **Understandable** | Users can understand | Clear language, consistent navigation |
| **Robust** | Works with assistive tech | Semantic HTML, ARIA labels |

**Critical Accessibility Checks:**

| Element | Requirement | How to Test |
|---------|-------------|-------------|
| **Color contrast** | 4.5:1 minimum for text | Use contrast checker tools |
| **Alt text** | All images must have descriptive alt | Inspect image tags |
| **Keyboard nav** | All interactive elements focusable | Tab through page |
| **Form labels** | All inputs must have labels | Screen reader test |
| **Link purpose** | Links must describe destination | No "click here" links |

**Contrast Ratios:**
```
WCAG AA Minimum:
- Normal text: 4.5:1
- Large text (18px+): 3:1
- UI components: 3:1

Common Failures:
- Light gray on white: ~2:1 ‚ùå
- Purple on dark purple: ~2.5:1 ‚ùå
- White on deep purple: ~8:1 ‚úÖ
```

**Rule:** Run every checkout page through WAVE or axe accessibility tools before launch.

### On Consistency
> "One wrong number in a proposal kills trust. Hero = Overview = Revenue Table = Footer. Every. Single. Number. Must. Match."

### On THE STACK
> "THE STACK is non-negotiable. Build value layer by layer, component by component, until price feels like a relief, not a risk. If they're not thinking 'that's it?!' at price reveal, your stack is too weak."

### On the 78% Rule
> "The sale happens BEFORE the webinar. Your job during the webinar is just to close what's already primed. 78% pre-consumption = 3-5X higher conversion."

### On Bonus Retention
> "'Keep the bonuses even if you cancel' reduces perceived risk to zero. It's not a giveaway ‚Äî it's a trust accelerator that increases conversions by removing the last objection."

### On Peak Commitment
> "The moment right after THE STACK reveal ‚Äî maximum value, minimum price ‚Äî is the peak emotional state. That's when your CTA must be STRONGEST, not weakest."

### On FTC Compliance
> "If a customer can miss the billing terms, you've already failed compliance. Clarity isn't just conversion ‚Äî it's legal protection."

### On Checkout Clarity
> "On checkout pages, clever copy kills conversions. Users are in decision mode. They need clarity, not creativity. If you have to explain what a button does, it's too clever."

### On Accessibility
> "Accessibility isn't charity ‚Äî it's 10-15% of your users. White on deep purple? That's 8:1 contrast and it converts. Light gray on white? That's 2:1, and you're losing sales and risking lawsuits."
