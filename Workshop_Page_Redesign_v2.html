Why The AI Sweet Spot Framework Is Used By The Top 5% Of Companies

While 95% of AI initiatives fail, companies using this framework consistently turn AI into measurable results. Here's why it works.

1. Strategy

Which workflows, if redesigned with automation & AI, would have the greatest impact on your growth this quarter? Define outcomes before selecting tools.

2. People

How do we prepare teams before deployment happens? The difference between adoption and resistance is preparation. Most companies skip this step.

3. Technology

Which tools actually solve our defined problems? Technology comes third, not first. This is where most companies start and fail.

4. Execution

How do we measure value at every stage? Each element builds on the last, ensuring outcomes drive decisions not the reverse.
